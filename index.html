About me

I am a researcher in computer vision and remote sensing, currently pursuing a PhD at the University of St. Gallen under the supervision of Damian Borth and the co-supervision of Michael Mommert and Ribana Roscher. My research focuses on building multimodal models for Earth observation, with an emphasis on implicit supervision and modality-aware learning. I previously worked as a Student Researcher at Google Research in Zürich and completed my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at EPFL. My work explores how physical, architectural, and modality-level signals can guide the learning in remote sensing systems.
